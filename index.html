Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our web development services. If you have any other queries, feel free to reach out!

1. How long does it take to build a website?

Depending on the complexity, a basic website can take 2-4 weeks. For more customized or complex sites, it may take 6-8 weeks or more.

2. Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we provide monthly website maintenance services to ensure your site remains up-to-date, secure, and running smoothly.

3. Can I update my website content on my own?

Absolutely! We can build your site on a user-friendly content management system (CMS) like WordPress, allowing you to make updates easily without needing coding skills.

4. What do I need to get started with a website?

To get started, we’ll need to discuss your goals, target audience, and desired features. Any branding materials, such as logos and content, will also be helpful.

5. What are the costs involved in developing a website?

Costs vary depending on the complexity of the website and the features you require. Our pricing starts from KES 20,000 for basic websites. Please reach out for a detailed quote.
